DRIVEN BY OAK,
DEFINED BY FLAVOR.

Smokehut isn’t a typical grill cafe. It's a sanctuary for the late-night fast food culture and gourmet grill lovers. We set out to challenge the ordinary by combining premium quality meats with modern, social-media-driven aesthetic presentations.

Every dish that leaves our hot-station is infused with the crisp aroma of traditional firewood smoke, handcrafted sauces, and perfectly balanced fusion ingredients. Made fresh, served bold.
